How we work

Four weeks, four quiet steps.

04  chapters
  1. i.

    Discover

    We listen · to you, your customers, your competitors. We agree on what the site has to do before deciding how it looks.

  2. ii.

    Design

    Mood boards, typography, palette, layout. You see a complete static preview before a single line of code is written.

  3. iii.

    Build

    Hand-coded, responsive, fast. Weekly review calls. You sign off section by section.

  4. iv.

    Launch

    Domain, hosting, analytics, training. You own everything from the first day · no lock-in, no monthly leash.
